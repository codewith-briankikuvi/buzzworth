Client Policy & Terms of Service

Our commitment to a transparent and professional partnership. Please review our policies below.

payments Payment Terms

We require a 50% upfront payment before commencing any project. This initial payment secures your spot in our schedule and allows us to allocate the necessary resources. The remaining 50% is due upon project completion, prior to the final delivery of files. Payments can be made via bank transfer, M-Pesa, or other mutually agreed-upon methods.

rate_review Revision Policy

We believe in collaboration and strive for your complete satisfaction. Each project includes up to three rounds of revisions. This ensures the final product aligns perfectly with your vision. Any additional revisions beyond this scope may be subject to extra charges, which will be discussed and agreed upon beforehand. To keep the project on track, we request that revision feedback be submitted within 7 days of receiving a design draft.

cancel Refund & Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your project within 24 hours of the initial payment for a full refund. Should you need to cancel after this period, a partial refund will be issued based on the amount of work already completed. Once the final designs have been approved and delivered, no refunds will be issued. We aim for a fair process that respects both your investment and our team's dedicated time.

cloud_upload File Delivery

Upon receipt of the final payment, all your project files will be organized and delivered electronically through a secure, private link. It is the client's responsibility to ensure they have the appropriate software to access and utilize these files. Unless a non-disclosure agreement is signed, we reserve the right to showcase the completed work in our portfolio and for other promotional purposes.
